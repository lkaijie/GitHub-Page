About

For some reason the main platform for artists I like is Twitter, and frankly, I really dislike the way Twitter does their home Feed. Your home feed is often plagued with retweets and tweets from people you don't follow, and it's annoying because I just want to see the tweets from the artists I follow. I created this discord bot to notify me whenever an artist I follow tweets, and it also allows me to view their tweets in a clean and simple way. This is my first personal project where I actually followed proper conventions, making use of .envs, workflows, proper file structure, proper commit messages and more.


Features

The main functionality of this bot is to notify users whenever an artist they follow media, sending a message to the channel they specified.

Invite link (The bot is self hosted, expect downtime)

Main commands

  • /add : Adds a twitter artist to track
  • /art_list : Shows currently tracked artists
  • /delete : Delete the specified artist
  • /transfer : Tracks all users you currently follow

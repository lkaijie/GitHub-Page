About

The main platform for artists nowadays seems to be Twitter, and frankly, I really dislike the way they do their home Feed. The home feed is often plagued with retweets and tweets from people you don't follow, and it's annoying because I am only interested in the tweets from the artists I follow. This discord bot was created to notify me whenever an artist I follow uploads media, it also allows me to view their tweets in a clean and simple way. This is my first personal project where I actually followed proper conventions, making use of .envs, workflows, proper file structure, proper commit messages and more.


Features

The main functionality of this bot is to notify users whenever an artist they follow media, sending a message to the channel they specified.

Invite link (The bot is self hosted, expect downtime)

Main commands

  • /add : Adds a twitter artist to track
  • /art_list : Shows currently tracked artists
  • /delete : Delete the specified artist
  • /transfer : Tracks all users you currently follow
